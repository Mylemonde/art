Studio A

Studio A is a Sydney-based social enterprise that supports artists with intellectual disabilities. The studio provides professional opportunities, exhibitions, and creative training so that artists can thrive and be recognised in contemporary culture.

We believe great art should be accessible to all. Studio A empowers artists to find their voice and create meaningful impact in the art world.
